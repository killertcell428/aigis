# Product Hunt Comment Templates

PH Maker信頼構築のための自然なコメント案。AI/セキュリティ/開発者ツール系プロダクトに対して使用。

## AI/LLMツール向け

### 1. 機能への共感
"Great work! The [specific feature] is exactly what I've been looking for when building LLM apps. How do you handle edge cases with [related topic]?"

### 2. セキュリティ観点の質問
"Congrats on the launch! Curious about security — do you have any guardrails for prompt injection or PII leakage? That's becoming a big concern for enterprise adoption."

### 3. 統合に関する質問
"Love the simplicity! Does this integrate with LangChain or LangGraph? I'm building an agent workflow and would love to plug this in."

## セキュリティツール向け

### 4. 技術的な賞賛
"This is really well thought out. The [specific detection approach] reminds me of how we approach LLM security — catching threats at the input/output layer before they reach the model."

### 5. 日本市場の視点
"Interesting approach! I work in the Japanese market where AI governance regulations (AI事業者ガイドライン) just got stricter. Tools like this are becoming essential."

## 開発者ツール向け

### 6. DX（開発者体験）への共感
"The developer experience looks great — being able to get started in just a few lines of code is so important. Too many security tools make you jump through hoops."

### 7. ゼロ依存への共感
"Love that this is zero-dependency / minimal setup. Dependency hell is real, especially in Python. Keeping it simple is an underrated feature."

### 8. オープンソースへの応援
"Congrats on the launch! I'm a big fan of open-source security tools — transparency is crucial when you're trusting something to protect your app."

## エージェント/自動化ツール向け

### 9. MCP関連
"Nice! Have you looked into MCP (Model Context Protocol) security? It's becoming a huge attack surface — 43% of MCP servers have injection vulnerabilities. Would love to see this integrated."

### 10. エンタープライズ視点
"This looks promising for enterprise adoption. The compliance reporting angle is something a lot of teams struggle with. Do you support any specific frameworks (OWASP, NIST, etc.)?"

---

## Usage Notes
- 1日2-3件ペースで投稿
- 自然な会話を心がけ、自ツールの宣伝は控える
- 質問形式でエンゲージメントを促す
- ローンチ4-5週前からMaker信頼スコアを構築
